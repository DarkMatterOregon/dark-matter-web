Who We Are

We are technologists that believe in innersourcing and team augmentation. Dark Matter consultants have a wide spectrum of skills to fit the missing elements in your company's tech story. Our founders Doug and Mark have deep histories with .Net and have been building products for a wide range of industries from large event management, food networks, supply-chains, criminal justice and wellness apps. Based in Eugene Oregon, all of our consultants are locally sourced from the talent pools of the local Universities and Colleges as well as seasoned experts. With team shaping, we can craft a skillset to best-fit your project’s technical needs and budget. We are community minded. We support local civic tech projects and provide upskilling to the local talent pipeline.

Blazor

We are one of the first teams delivering Blazor Server and Web Assembly Apps in production. Web Assembly opens up a new world of web development. It’s not often that a game-changing technology comes along that radically simplifies development like Blazor. We might seem like risk-takers at the moment, but Blazor is not an experiment. It’s baked into .Net and has a bright future with a very long tail.

Azure

While we have clients who use AWS, deploying .NET apps into Microsoft's Azure Cloud platform is simple, flexible, and affordable.

Oqtane

Oqtane is a modular application framework for Blazor, and Dark Matter is an early adopter. If your application requires a content management system (CMS), then Oqtane may be a good solution for you. With built in User, Role, and Page management, as well as support for custom modules designed by Dark Matter, you can be up and running with a new system in weeks.
